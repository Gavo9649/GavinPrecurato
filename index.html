NHRL Combat Robot | YSU Robotics Club | Dec 2025 - Apr 2026

30 Pound Combat Robot Design

A mechanical design project centered on packaging, durability, weight management, and the performance tradeoffs required for a competition robot.

Selected Work

  • Designed an NHRL combat robot within a 30 pound competition weight constraint.
  • Selected materials based on durability and expected loading during competition.
  • Applied dynamics coursework to estimate energy transfer and system speed.
  • Designed parts for additive and traditional manufacturing, reducing non-critical part weight by up to 10% of the overall robot weight.

3D Printed NERF Blaster | Jan 2025

Additively Manufactured Blaster Assembly

A rapid-prototyping project focused on CAD assembly modeling, motion checks, tolerance iteration, and fast validation through 3D printed parts.

Selected Work

  • Designed an elastic-band-powered NERF blaster with over 93% 3D printed components.
  • Modeled the full assembly in CAD to analyze motion, failure points, and tolerances.
  • Used rapid prototyping to move part testing from days to hours.
  • Iterated around printability, assembly fit, and validation speed.

3D Printed BMO MTG Card Box | Jun 2025

Printed Card Box with Embedded Electronics

A playful enclosure project that blends 3D printed product design with embedded electronics packaging, controls, charging, and hand-built perfboard wiring.

Selected Work

  • Designed a BMO-inspired Magic: The Gathering card box as a functional printed enclosure.
  • Packaged a battery, USB-C battery charger, ESP32, buttons, and wiring into the available internal volume.
  • Combined the electronics on a perfboard PCB to keep the assembly compact and serviceable.
  • Balanced card storage, charging access, power switch access, and printability in the enclosure layout.
